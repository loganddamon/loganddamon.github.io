THE MAN BEHIND THE COMPANY


Sir Logan Damon was born in Fairbanks, Alaska at the turn of the millennium. It was clear from an early age that Sir Logan’s capabilities would only be eclipsed by his ambition to realize them.

By the age of 15, he had already registered a dozen patents in a wide range of fields from biotech to robotics, including his dynamic breakthroughs in generating a synthetic atmosphere for use above the polar ice cap in 2025.

In the next decade, Damon Corporation will become a worldwide leader in emerging technologies and launch the first privatized industrial mission to leave the planet Earth. "There are other worlds than this one," Sir Logan boldly declared, "And if there is no air to breathe, we will simply have to make it."
